About

Wenda Wang is currently a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Zhewei at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. Prior to this, he obtained a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics from the School of Mathematics, Renmin University of China in June 2024, and a Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Shandong University in June 2021.

Research Interests

His research interests lie in AI for Science, with a special focus on protein design and molecule generation, and LLM-based molecular understanding. He is dedicated to leveraging large language models and generative methods to advance drug discovery and molecular science. He aims to explore how computational approaches can accelerate the understanding and engineering of biomolecules.
